What we're building

We partner with owners, investors, and hands‑on builders to revive healthy but stuck companies. We focus the first 120 days on operational diagnostics, cash clarity, team momentum, and a simple plan that actually gets done.

  • Operator cohort (3 months): sprint prep for the on‑site embed — tooling, playbooks, case reviews, and rep‑heavy practice.
  • On‑site embed: the operator parachutes in with a 30‑60‑90 and a sponsor network. Tight advisory cadence, weekly metrics, monthly resets.
  • Outcomes: stabilized cash, simplified ops, renewed shipping culture, and a path to durable profitability.
